pasta_and_garlic

Description

On this page you will find all the ingredients to cook a delicous meal for at least 8 servings.
This meal is very fast to cook and easy to make. Below you find a list will all the ingredients and how to put them all together. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1½ pounds pasta
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed, or more to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot of salted water boil pasta until al dente. Drain well.
  2. In a small saucepan heat oil slightly, add garlic. Saute garlic until it is lightly browned. Be careful not to burn garlic.
  3. Combine pasta and garlic, salt and pepper to taste. Top with Parmesan or Romano cheese and serve.
